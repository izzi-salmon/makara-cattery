Makara Cattery Terms and Conditions

1. Bookings and Payment

Bookings are essential and subject to availability.

Current pricing

  • $20 per day per cat for standard periods.
  • $25 per day per cat during December and January (Christmas period) for 2024 and 2025.

Price increase 2026

  • From January 1st 2026 standard daily rate will increase to $25 per day per cat.
  • December 2026 and January 2027 (Christmas period), rate will increase to $30 per day per cat.

Over the Christmas period (December and January), there is a minimum booking charge of 10 days per cat. You do not need to stay the full 10 days but will be charged a minimum of $250 per cat during this period.

Full payment is due on the day of collection, unless paid earlier by arrangement.

Accepted payment methods include bank transfer, cash, or approved electronic payment services.

A non-refundable deposit may be required for peak periods or long-term stays.

Late pick-ups outside of standard hours will incur an additional $20 standard fee, or $50 during December and January (Christmas period).

2. Veterinary Care

At Makara Cattery, the health and safety of your cat is our top priority. If your cat becomes unwell or requires veterinary attention during their stay:

  • We will promptly seek treatment, with your cat's welfare always coming first.
  • We have a longstanding and trusted 20+ year relationship with Karori Vet, our closest and most direct veterinary partner. Unless alternative arrangements are made in advance, Karori Vet will be used for all veterinary needs.

A transport fee will apply:

  • $20 each way for visits to Karori Vet
  • $40 each way for vets outside the Karori area

All veterinary treatment costs are the responsibility of the cat's owner. We will attempt to contact you before proceeding with non-urgent treatment; however, in an emergency, we reserve the right to act in the best interest of your pet.

3. Medication

Medication can be administered as instructed:

  • $5 per morning session
  • $5 per evening session

All medications must be clearly labeled with the cat's name and dosage instructions.

We reserve the right to decline care if a condition or treatment is beyond our safe handling capability.

4. Feeding

We provide Iams dry biscuits as a standard base diet.

If your cat requires special or vet-prescribed food, please bring enough for the entire stay, clearly labeled with feeding instructions.

Fresh water is provided at all times.

5. Bedding and Belongings

We supply all bedding, blankets, water bowls, food bowls, and litter trays.

Due to hygiene and safety, personal items are not permitted, as most cats do not engage with them and may become distressed by unfamiliar scents.

6. Abandonment Policy

Cats must be collected on the agreed pick-up date.

If your cat is not collected within 7 days and no contact has been made, we will attempt to reach you using the contact information provided.

If there is no communication or collection within 30 days, your cat will be considered abandoned.

At 6 weeks, we reserve the right to begin rehoming your cat, unless other arrangements have been confirmed.

7. Health Requirements

All cats must be deflead prior to arrival.

All cats must be currently vaccinated (F3 or F4).

Vaccination records must be provided at the first visit and updated annually thereafter.

Cats must be wormed, in good health, and free of contagious conditions.

We reserve the right to refuse entry to any cat that appears unwell, has fleas, or poses a risk to others in our care.

Owners must disclose any medical conditions, allergies, or behavioural issues at the time of booking.

8. Operating Hours

Open 7 days a week:

  • Morning: 8:30 AM – 11:00 AM
  • Afternoon: 3:00 PM – 6:00 PM

Closed on:

  • Christmas Day (25 December)
  • Boxing Day (26 December)
  • New Year's Day (1 January)
  • 18 August – 28 August 2025

Any additional closed dates will be clearly blocked out in the booking calendar.

Out-of-hours collections/drop-offs are strictly by prior arrangement and will incur fees:

  • $20 standard out-of-hours fee
  • $50 out-of-hours fee during December and January

9. Personal Information Requirements

Before each stay, we require the following up-to-date information:

  • Owner's full name
  • Best contact telephone number
  • Email address
  • Name of your regular veterinary clinic
  • Date of your cat's next vaccination

Agreement that Karori Vet will be used for all veterinary needs unless an alternative is specified and agreed upon in advance.

10. Liability and Care

While we take every precaution, Makara Cattery accepts no liability for illness, injury, escape, or death due to natural causes, pre-existing conditions, or stress-related health issues.

All veterinary fees and related costs incurred during your cat's stay are the responsibility of the owner.

Owners agree to indemnify Makara Cattery from any claims resulting from their cat's behaviour, health, or condition.

11. Cancellations

Cancellations made within 48 hours of drop-off may incur a fee.
